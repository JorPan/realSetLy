What is SetLy?

SetLy is a place for DJ's and performing Producers to manage their song library and keep track of shows they play to gain valuable insights on their performances:

  1. - What songs they've played and how frequently,
  2. - How many tickets they've sold,
  3. - Build set lists for future shows based on their song library.



Concert Goers can create accounts to share setlists with friends, keep track of shows they've attended, and get data like how many times they've seen an artist perform their favorite song.
